Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Bookie is an all-in-one event management and ticketing platform designed to simplify the process of organizing, promoting, and managing events.

Bookie offers tools for event creation, promotion, ticket sales, and attendee management, making it easier for organizers to handle all aspects of their events.

Yes, Bookie supports both online and in-person events.

Event Creation

To create an event, sign in to your Bookie account, click on the "Create Event" button, and follow the prompts to enter event details, ticket types, pricing, and other relevant information.

During the event creation process, you can set up multiple ticket types by specifying names, prices, and quantity available.

Ticket Sales

Tickets are delivered to attendees via email.

Attendees can purchase tickets directly from the event page on the Bookie platform.

Attendee Management

You can view and manage attendee information through the Bookie dashboard, where you can track ticket sales, contact attendees, and export attendee lists.

Payment and Fees

Bookie is free.

Support and Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, you can contact our customer support team through the Bookie help center or by emailing support@bookie.com. We offer 24/7 support to assist you with any problems.
